About Me

Hello! I am a cognitive scientist, behavioral researcher, and data analyst.

Across scientific and consumer/market research roles, I have over 10 years of experience in experimental design, statistical analysis, data visualization, automatic text analysis, and qualitative methods. I love turning data into insights and figuring out how best to clearly communicate my findings.

I received my PhD in cognitve psychology and an MS in statistics from Northwestern University in June 2023. My scientific research interests include analogical reasoning, language processing, and metaphor comprehension.

I'm now seeking to use my research and data analysis training in a career in data analytics / data science.
